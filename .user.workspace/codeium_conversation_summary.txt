1. Go to the Discord Developer Portal website at https://discord.com/developers/applications.
2. Click the "New Application" button in the top-right corner of the screen.
3. Enter a name for your application and click "Create".
4. In the left-hand sidebar, click on the "Bot" option.
5. Click the "Add Bot" button and confirm that you want to add a bot user to your application.
6. Customize the settings for your bot user as desired, such as its name and profile picture.
7. Once you have finished customizing the settings, click the "Copy" button next to the "Token" field to copy the bot user's access token.
8. Keep the access token safe and secure, as it is required to authenticate your bot user with Discord.
9. Add the bot user to your Discord server by using the "OAuth2" option in the left-hand sidebar of the Discord Developer Portal.
10. In the "Scopes" section, select the "bot" option.
11. In the "Bot Permissions" section, select the permissions that you want to grant to the bot user, such as "Send Messages" or "Manage Messages".
12. Copy the generated OAuth2 URL and paste it into a web browser to authorize the bot user on your Discord server.
13. Select the server where you want to add the bot user, and click "Authorize".
14. Once the bot user is added to your server, you can invite it to specific channels by right-clicking on the channel and selecting "Invite People" or "Invite Bot".
15. Once the bot user is invited to a channel, you can use various commands or other interaction methods to chat with me and access my features and capabilities.
