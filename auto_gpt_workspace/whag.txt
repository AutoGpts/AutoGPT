Whag is a platform designed to provide online security and privacy for content creators, business owners, and everyday web users. Its key features include Verified Links, Whitelists, a Community Blacklist, and Whag Tags, which are verifiable digital identities. The platform uses Distributed Ledger Technology to create Verified Digital Identities and links with provenance. Whag's Chrome Extension offers Protection Mode to block access to dangerous or malicious links, and users can choose from three levels of protection based on their needs. Whag offers a free 30-day trial, and its pricing plans are simple and transparent. The platform aims to build trust online and empower users to take control of their digital identity.
